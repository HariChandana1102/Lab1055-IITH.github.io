For Women Students/PhD Graduates

For women students who are interested in doing a PhD or postdoc with us, please consider applying for the WISE PhD and Postdoctoral Fellowships at https://dst.gov.in/scientific-programmes/wise-kiran. Applications are accepted round the year. For PhD graduates wanted to join us for a postdoc, we'd expect at least one CORE A* (or A)-ranked publication during your PhD.
